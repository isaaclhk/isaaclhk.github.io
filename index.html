AI Engineering for Healthcare

Hi there, and thanks for visiting my page. I’m an Associate AI Engineer at AI Singapore under the AI Apprenticeship Programme, and I’m currently pursuing Georgia Tech’s OMSA. My work centers on applying machine learning, statistical modelling, and MLOps to real-world problems, with a focus on healthcare AI. Previously, I worked as a registered cardiology nurse, which gave me firsthand insight into clinical workflows and the challenges of data-driven decision-making in healthcare. Today, I’m especially interested in building reliable, interpretable, and well-calibrated models that can support clinicians and improve patient outcomes. This blog is where I share projects and technical notes on translating data and ML into meaningful impact in healthcare and beyond.
